Features

Be Inspired. Explore our vast collection of world-class resorts and resort destinations across continents.


The Sani Resort


The Sani Resort in Halidiki, Greece is a 5* child friendly resort with a range of different accommodation options including a hotel and apartments. It is set on a 1000 acre eco reserve surrounded by forests, olive groves and a stunning sandy beach.

Paradise Cove Resort


Highly romantic, Paradise Cove resort in Mauritius offers ‘love nest’ twin sun beds – a wonderful way to soak up the sun! Nestled in beautiful tropical gardens, Paradise Cove is a gorgeous adults-only boutique hotel, offering guests a secluded retreat from the world.

Shangri-La Boracay Resort


Somewhere in the Maldives accessible by boat, the Shangri_La Boracay shows off the sea from a posh perpective. For privacy and for a sense of space, this is the right spot.

Beaches Ocho Rios Resort


For active families who like to be out and about, Ocho Rios in Jamaica offers an array of facilities; from Kids' Camps with Sesame games for kids, Scratch DJ Academy for teenagers; dining options and a Red Lane Spa for adults; as well as an extended Pirates Island Waterpark with 11 water slides that will enthuse everyone.
